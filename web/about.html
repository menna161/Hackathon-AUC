About Us

Public health improvement is the most important field which must be put into consideration to ensure the safety of the citizens. But lately, public health in Egypt has been plagued with problems and inconveniences. This is mainly due to; over-crowdedness in public hospitals, limited number of equipped baby incubators and intensive care units and. These are some of the major factors playing havoc with public health in our country. But the main and most important problem is the over-crowdedness in hospitals and the inconveniences that occur due to full hospitals. When emergencies happen and people try to contact hospitals, they fail to do so and they have to go to the hospital just to find that it’s full and they have to search for another one. This only wastes much needed time and puts people’s lives at risk. The current solution for this problem is contacting the hospital or calling an ambulance, but with the huge population and limited medical facilities, this usually doesn’t work. The prior solution was a hotline that can be called to inquire about empty places in hospitals, but this didn’t prove to be efficient due to overpopulation.
Characteristics for a successful solution would be based on the identified design requirements is to be efficient, cost effective, its materials are available, safe to human and environment, accurate and sustainable. Based on these requirements the solution is a website that has information about all hospitals such as type, address, phone number and most importantly empty places in the hospital at this instant. it is easy to construct and the system supplies the website with the accurate data. This system helps citizens to find the right hospitals and saves their time, thus saving people’s lives and reducing the annual number of deaths.
